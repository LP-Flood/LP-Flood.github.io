2. If You’re in a Flood Zone, Here’s What You Need

Required Permits

  • Floodplain Development Permit
  • Elevation Certificate (before foundation poured)
  • Building Permit with floodproofing plan

Development Prohibitions

  • No new residential structures below Base Flood Elevation
  • No fill or grading that alters natural floodplain drainage
  • No storage of hazardous materials within the floodway

Full ordinance details in our Floodplain Management Ordinance.
